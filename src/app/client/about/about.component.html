What we are

We are dynamic team of creative people

Human'Sup is a training school dedicated to transforming the theory and practice of humanitarian action. The school intends to train the next generation of humanitarians in the Global South with a view to changing the operational landscape in humanitarian contexts. The institution is at the forefront of academic debates on the future of humanitarian action and seeks to cultivate interdisciplinary exchanges, ideas and innovative practices in the humanitarian space. The school draws on a range of expertise, primarily giving a voice to critiques and voices from the Global South in order to equip students to be agents of change. It operates from Dakar, Senegal through face-to-face and online programs. The Human'Sup training offers include specialized training for students and junior professionals as well as for experienced professionals looking to advance their careers. The teaching tracks, part-time and online, are designed to meet the needs of students and professionals by providing them with the skills and tools they need to meet current and future challenges.
