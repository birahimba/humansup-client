GRADUATION DIPLOMA

Master Degree

LEARNING METHODS

Face to Face and Online

DURATION OF TRAINING

12 Months

OBJECTIVES OF THE MASTER

- Equip students and professionals with practical knowledge to develop, implement, coordinate, monitor and evaluate humanitarian projects and programmes.
- Provides students with knowledge of the dynamics of humanitarian and complex emergencies and engages critically with the history, politics and practice of humanitarian action.
- Offers a solid foundation on the humanitarian-development-peace nexus including the practical methods to achieve this in different contexts.
- Training in line with the demands of the moment and the evolution of the professional humanitarian action.
- Guide and support students in identifying their choice of (re)vocational integration.
- Assists students build a strong professional network.


TARGET

Professionals and students with an interest in humanitarian affairs and wishing to pursue a career in the humanitarian sector.


TRAININGS

CU1 - HUMANITARIAN PRINCIPLES AND ARCHITECTURE.
CU2 - HUMANITARIAN COORDINATION.

CU5 - CASH AND MARKET BASED PROGRAMMING IN CRISIS SETTINGS.
CU6 - MANAGING COMPLEX EMERGENCIES (SIMULATIONS) .
CU7 - CRITIQUES OF THE HUMANITARIAN SYSTEM .

SEMESTER 2 :

CU8 - DEVELOPMENT ECONOMICS.
CU9 - HUMANITARIAN-DEVELOPMENT-PEACE NEXUS
CU10 - NEXUS PROGRAMMING.
